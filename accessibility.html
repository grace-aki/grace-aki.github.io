Our Promise

This website is for anyone who wants to learn more about kalo. It was built so everyone can easily find what they're looking for, no matter what platform they're using. Information should always be easily accessible without barriers or confusion.

What I Worked On

Completed the following features to enhance accessibility:

  • Alt text on pics – every picture has descriptive words.
  • Headings that make sense – page flows in order, easy to follow and skip around.
  • Color contrasts – texts stand out from the background, making it easier to read.
  • Plays nice on all platforms – accessible on phones, tablets, desktop. Works even without a mouse, everything is reachable with a keyboard and can be tabbed to.
  • Forms you can actually use – every box lets you know what to type and accessible for screen readers.

How I Checked

Some ways I tested:

  • WAVE tool – checked everything with it and fixed what it caught.
  • Lighthouse – tested with DevTools and scored 93.
  • Keyboard only – clicked through everything w/ no mouse.
  • Screen reader – listened to the pages on the website to make sure it sounded right.

What I'm Still Working On

Currently in progress:

  • Better descriptions on my images.
  • A few images might not be floating correctly depending on the device.
  • Improved contrast quality on website.

See Something Off?

Please report any errors. This website is a work in progress and your feedback helps make it better. Use the Contact form or email akigrace@hawaii.edu. I'll try to get to it as quick as I can.
